The Ransom Note!

My beautiful girlfriend Annē (top left) and her twin sister Hannah (top right) celebrated their 30th birthday in theatrical style in March. Cleverly dubbed "The Ransom Note" (their last name is Ransom), the cocktail party/Broadway revue was a spectacular event. Talented friends took the stage to sing Broadway numbers and the show was wonderful.

I, among others, felt as though we should have been charged admission on account of how great the show was!
